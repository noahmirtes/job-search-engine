*This is a document to centralize random information I use / need during the developent process of this project.*

This is a local job search decision engine that replaces manual job board browsing with a structured pipeline. It pulls job listings from aggregated sources, stores everything historically, scores each job based on fit, and outputs a curated list of roles worth applying to.

HOW IT WORKS:
Jobs are fetched from an API (Google Jobs via SerpApi), and every request/response is stored. Jobs are normalized and deduplicated, but raw data is always preserved.
Each job is automatically scored using a mix of rule-based signals (keywords, title, seniority, etc.) and embedding similarity against your resume and an “ideal job” reference.
Exports generate a spreadsheet of only new, unseen jobs from the latest ingestion window that meet a minimum score threshold. This becomes your working list of jobs to apply to.
